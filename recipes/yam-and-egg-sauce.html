Yam and Egg Sauce

How to prepare yam and egg sauce

Description

Yam and egg sauce is a a popular meal prepared in most Nigerian homes. The modest cost for preparing it makes it such an excellent choice for many individuals and families. Also, preparing it doesn't require so much time making it an easy choice for very busy parents and individuals.

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Egg
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Get a tuber of yam and cut into appropriate sizes.
  2. Put them into a sizable pot and boil for 30 mins.
  3. Chop some tomatoes into very small pieces.
  4. Cut some onions also.
  5. Chop some pepper(depends on cook choice).
  6. Fry the onions for 3 mins in hot oil.
  7. Add the chopped tomatoes and pepper and fry for 10 mins.
  8. Break some eggs and add into the fry pan and fry with the mixture for 5 mins.
  9. Add some salt and seasoning to taste.
  10. Bon Appetit!
